Unbred/Male Heat

Every wolf, regardless of sex, has a heat cycle! It doesn't show on male wolves, but it coorelates to their age. Depending on their age, a male wolf might go immediately into heat when they're sex changed. This calculator will tell you if your male wolf would be in heat if you were to sex change him, or how far away his heat would be!
Using an aging crystal on an adolescent messes with this heat cycle.

*Button to check if they used an aging crystal and box to fill with at what age they did.*

*Function returns if the wolf will be in heat or not and what day of their heat they will be on, or how many days it will be until their heat.*
